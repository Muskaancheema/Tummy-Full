Zero HungerEvents

In these events we distribute food to the Needed individuals.

Gurugram,Haryana

Organized By TummyFull

Gurugram Community Meal Drive : In Gurugram, Tummyfull organizes a large-scale Community Meal Drive, where local businesses and volunteers come together to prepare and distribute meals to underprivileged communities. This event focuses on providing nutritious food to those in need, with an emphasis on supporting families and individuals in low-income neighborhoods. The drive also includes educational workshops on nutrition and healthy eating habits.

Delhi

Organized By TummyFull

Delhi Food Donation Fair : Tummyfull hosts the Delhi Food Donation Fair, an event that brings together donors, food banks, and community organizations. During this fair, surplus food from restaurants, supermarkets, and individuals is collected and redistributed to various shelters and food distribution centers across Delhi. The event also features awareness campaigns about food waste reduction and the importance of sustainable food practices.

Faridabad,Haryana

Organized By TummyFull

Faridabad Hunger Relief Fest : The Faridabad Hunger Relief Fest is a vibrant event organized by Tummyfull to address food insecurity in the region. This festival includes a food distribution program, where meals are served to local residents in need. The event also features activities such as cooking demonstrations, nutrition seminars, and community engagement activities to raise awareness about hunger issues and encourage collective action.
